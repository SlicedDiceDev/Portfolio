Ransacked

What Is Ransakced?

Ransacked is a first-person 4-player cooperative dungeon-crawling collectathon game, featuring role specialization through player development in puzzle-solving skills and light stealth-based gameplay to navigate past dangerous enemies you cannot fight, in an attempt to extract as much treasure as you can from the dungeon. These treasures throughout the randomly-generated dungeon space, alongside deeper access points into the dungeon, are blocked off behind locked doors which can only be unlocked with the correct tools, these tools giving the player access to their own tool-specific minigame to solve.
