Ruins

A map based on the design elements seen in Riot Games: VALORANT that would have its own unique twist while incorporating the same principles that are seen across all of their maps.

The intent of RUINS is to give players a new map to explore that feels similar to all previous maps, but still provide a learning experience that helps separate this map from the others with its own uniqueness.

Based on ancient temples and ruins of Cambodia, RUINS would be located in the Cambodian jungle with architecture and foliage similar to what is seen in the real life inspiration.
